## Community Projects
Due to ZenithForge solely being an API server, the community has provided custom-tailored applications to communicate with a ZenithForge backend.

> [!NOTE]
> If you would like to showcase a community project, feel free to contact a dev!

Below is a list of historical projects associated with the upstream ecosystem. Their names remain unchanged here because they are third-party project names, not ZenithForge branding:
- [vllmAPI Gradio Loader](https://github.com/lesj0610/vllmAPI-gradio-loader) by Doctor Shotgun - Historical upstream WebUI for loading and unloading models and loras.
- [ST tabbyAPI Loader](https://github.com/theroyallab/ST-tabbyapi-loader) by kingbri - Historical SillyTavern extension for loading and unloading models.
- [SillyTavern Launcher](https://github.com/SillyTavern/SillyTavern-Launcher) by deffcolony - CLI manager for AI tools that may be adapted for ZenithForge later.
- [SillyTavern](https://github.com/SillyTavern/SillyTavern) by Cohee and RossAscends - AI frontend reference for future ZenithForge compatibility testing.
- [Agnaistic](https://agnai.chat/) by Sceik - Multi-user AI role-play chat frontend reference.
- [DS-LLM-WebUI](https://github.com/DocShotgun/ds-llm-webui) by Doctor Shotgun - Tool-use assistant UI from the upstream ecosystem.
- [PolyMind](https://github.com/itsme2417/PolyMind) by Itsme2417 - Multimodal function-calling UI reference.
